MY WORK, DISPLAYED

GRATEFUL DELT

Inspired by the Grateful Dead, Delta Tau Delta's winter hoodie design quickly became my most popular piece with over 230 sales. This piece has been released twice in three colorways of yellow, baby blue, and black.

TONIE ARCON

Jackson Michigan's "Tonie Arcon" is a hip-hop rap artist by night and a investment banker working on Wall Street by day. This design was inspired by his dual personality and the idea of "The Way", a mentality he preaches about recognizing the natural flow of life.

EARRING DESIGNS

These earring designs were created as a final project for my sophomore studio course "Bits and Atoms". These designs were designed in Rhinocerous and 3d resin printed to scale.

WELCOME WEEK "CHAMPIONS"

This design created for Delta Tau Delta was inspired by vintage Rose Bowl designs which are vastly popular accross campus today. When designing for Greek Life, it is important to reflect modern trends in order to maximize interest and sales.

FALL RUSH 2021

This tee design was created for the current fall rush season, and was inspired by a color pallete reflecting fall trends.

PANK HOODIES

This hoodie design was created for a group of artists from Minnesota. These artists pride themselves in being "against the machine" and frequently oppose constucts and the law. This idea is reflected in the design which depicts the "key to the city", in which questioning present systems can unlock new mindsets.

DTD x PIPHI

As a collaborative effort between Delta Tau Delta and Pi Beta Phi, this piece was created in support of Relay for Life. Portions of the cost of each piece sold were donated to charity.

FALL RUSH 2020

As my first design as the merchandise chair, I depicted a sunset scene using the colors of purple and yellow which reflect our chapter's identity. This design went through many iterations and is one of my favorites as it depicts the house we live in next to the shore.

WINTER RUSH 2020

As part of our winter rush process, merchandise is created for the brothers. This design was hand drawn and depicts a ski scene printed on a ice blue longsleeve that evokes the season of winter.

DTD QUARANTINE

During the peak of quarantine, there were almost zero activities through the fraternity. This design promoted involvement during a time where it was really tricky to, and made light of a trying time.

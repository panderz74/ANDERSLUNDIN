ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Anders, an undergraduate junior dual majoring at the University of Michigan studying art/design within the Stamps School of Art and Design and User Experience within the School of Information.

While I grew my portfolio centered around photography, I have grown to define myself as an interdisciplinary creator curious about the intersections of art, information, and technology.

After my education, I hope to work with various artist within the music industry while continuing to develop myself as an interdisciplinary creator. From music videos to social media to brand identity, I see myself working alongside musical visionaries to create content that touches those who experience it.
